Nine Square

Red Turn

Goal: Move all your red or blue pieces to the opponent's corner (3x3 area)

Controls: Click to select pieces, click green squares to move/jump

Jumping: Jump over any piece (yours or opponent's) to move faster across the board

Strategy: Use jumps to reach the opponent's corner quickly, or click "End Turn" to stop mid-jump sequence
